Your Digital Footprint

A Digital footprint is the trace you leave on the internet with all the posts and whatnot you would upload on social media. Your digital footprint can have a massive impact on your future, with things like potential jobs.

Avoid putting hateful and or inappropriate comments or posts
-such as things like revealing photos of your body, or the wrong way to put your opinion out there for certain things like political issues or just small things like small-time celebrities.
Keep everything private
-simply just don't have anything on your social media at all.
Think before you post

Final remarks

With the information described in videos I've seen and what I've learned about through social media, no matter what you put on the internet, it can be found. If there was one thing I could tell you, it would be to never post anything political, or inappropriate on the internet.
